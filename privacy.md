# Privacy Policy for QuoteRank Extension

**Last updated: April 2026**

This privacy policy applies to the Chrome Extension created by [YOUR NAME OR COMPANY] strictly for internal team use.

### 1. Data Collection
Because this is an internal business tool, the Extension collects very limited data required for it to function:
* **Website Content (URLs):** When you visit X.com (Twitter), the extension reads the URL of the specific post you are viewing to extract the Post ID. 
* **User Activity (Clicks):** The extension tracks when a user clicks on a quote post inside the extension's sidebar module. 

### 2. How We Use Data
* The Post ID is sent to our internal backend to retrieve the top quote posts for that specific post.
* Click data is sent to our internal backend solely to measure how often the team utilizes the tool and which quotes are engaged with.

### 3. Data Sharing and Sale
We **do not** sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. This tool and its data are strictly for internal company/team use.
